Pepperoni Pizza Recipe

Description

Pepperoni pizza is a classic American favorite featuring a crispy crust topped with zesty tomato sauce, melted mozzarella cheese, and plenty of spicy pepperoni slices. It's a crowd-pleaser that's perfect for parties, game nights, or a casual meal.

Ingredients

  • Pizza dough (store-bought or homemade)
  • Tomato sauce (pizza or marinara)
  • Mozzarella cheese (shredded)
  • Pepperoni slices
  • Olive oil
  • Salt and pepper
  • Dried oregano and garlic powder (optional)

Steps

  1. Preheat Oven: Preheat your oven to 475°F (245°C). If using a pizza stone, place it in the oven to heat.
  2. Prepare Dough: Roll out the dough on a floured surface into a round or rectangular shape.
  3. Add Sauce: Spread tomato sauce evenly over the dough, leaving a small edge for the crust.
  4. Add Cheese and Toppings: Sprinkle mozzarella cheese, then evenly layer pepperoni slices on top. Season with oregano or garlic powder if desired.
  5. Bake: Bake for 10–15 minutes until the crust is golden and the cheese is bubbly and slightly browned.
  6. Serve: Let it cool for a few minutes, then slice and enjoy your pepperoni pizza.
